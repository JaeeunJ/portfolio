Rendezvoo

This project was for the 2022 McMaster Designathon.
The team designed a tool that allows organizations to easily plan and manage events while taking into account employees' schedules. My roles for this project were UI/UX Design, Prototyping, User Testing, and Branding.

Date

November 04 - 06, 2022

Tools

Figma, Figjam

The Team

3 product designers

See Prototype

SEE FIGMA PROTOTYPE
01. the problem

The lack of a method to easily plan and manage events while considering all employees.

The current system of locating classrooms at the university often requires the usage of static, inconspicuous floor plans, whose unintuitive nature leads to students getting lost and wasting time using inefficient routes.

There are many pain points, including a lack of contact due to the pandemic caused a shift in company culture. It is also diffcult to accomodate all employees. Finally, it is tedious to plan trips due to the many considerations.

02. our solution

Create a mobile application for employers to manage trips and employees to respond with ease.

05. design system

Palette

To add a professional and elegant touch to the application, we decided to use a contrasting mix of navy blue and white. A dash of pink for contrast and a bit of casual flavour!

Typography

We decided to use both a serif and a sans-serif typography to give it a professional base with a slightly more casual flair.
